## Notes for Reviewers

- The app contains an AI chat feature, but it does not generate inappropriate content. The AI is instructed to be a productivity assistant focused on task management and organization.
- All user content is private and not shared with other users.
- There is no social/community feature, no user-to-user messaging, and no public profiles.
- The app does not contain any in-app purchases in the current version.
