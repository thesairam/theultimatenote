## Google Play — Full Description (4000 chars max)

```
Plan. Prioritize. Learn. Journal. Grow.

The Ultimate Note is the productivity app that's exactly enough — not too much like Notion, not too little like a basic to-do list. It combines Kanban boards, an Eisenhower priority matrix, daily planning, journaling, and an AI assistant into one clean, calm experience.

KANBAN BOARDS
Create projects with fully customizable Kanban boards. Drag tasks between columns, track progress visually, and stay on top of every project — from side hustles to life goals.

EISENHOWER PRIORITY MATRIX
Switch between Kanban and Matrix view with one tap. See your tasks organized by urgency and importance across four quadrants: Do First, Schedule, Quick Win, and Low Priority. Move tasks between quadrants to reprioritize instantly.

DAILY PLANNER
Your morning command center. See today's recurring tasks, temporary to-dos, and learning goals in one place. Set specific reminder times and get notified when it's time to act. Recurring tasks automatically reset each day.

LEARNING TRACKER
A dedicated space for managing your learning paths. Track courses, skills, and study sessions with their own Kanban board. See today's learning goals alongside your daily tasks.

JOURNALS & NOTEBOOKS
Every project gets its own notebook. Write planning notes, progress logs, or personal journal entries with clean formatting — headings, bold, italics, lists, and more. Create additional notebooks for any purpose.

AI ASSISTANT
Chat with an AI assistant that knows your projects and tasks. Brainstorm ideas, get suggestions, and let the AI create tasks and projects for you right from the conversation.

MOTIVATION
Set up your inspirational figures in your profile. Get daily motivational quotes delivered as push notifications to start your morning right.

DESIGNED TO FEEL RIGHT
Deep emerald and burnished gold. Smooth rounded cards. Subtle borders. The Ultimate Note is designed to feel calm, trustworthy, and productive — a space where you want to spend time organizing your life.

KEY FEATURES:
- Kanban boards with customizable columns
- Eisenhower priority matrix (urgent/important)
- Switch between Kanban and Matrix views
- Daily recurring and temporary tasks
- Scheduled task reminders with notifications
- Learning path tracker
- Rich-text notebooks and journaling
- AI chat assistant (powered by Groq + Gemini)
- Google Sign-In authentication
- Cloud sync across devices (Firebase)
- Offline-first — works without internet
- Beautiful deep emerald and gold theme

PRIVACY FIRST
Your data stays in your Firebase account. We don't sell your data, we don't track you for ads, and we don't share your information with third parties. Period.

Plan. Prioritize. Learn. Journal. Grow.
```
